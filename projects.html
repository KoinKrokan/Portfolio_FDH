Analysis of the Regional Structure of South Sumatra Province Using Gravity Data & Interpretaions For Oil And Gas

Geophysics Geology Structure
Geosain Delta Andalan
Key Responsibilities
  • Collected Supporting Data
  • Gravity Data Processing
  • Gravity Interpretations
  • Regional Structure Analysis

Analysis of regional geological structures in South Sumatra Province utilizes gravity data to support oil and gas exploration. Gravity anomalies are interpreted to delineate key subsurface features such as sedimentary basins, structural highs, and fault zones. Integration of geological maps and well data improves interpretation accuracy, contributing to a better understanding of the tectonic framework and identification of potential hydrocarbon prospects.

Geostatistical Modeling Of Environmental Pollution From Palm Oil Waste

Enviromental Geology Geostatistics
Madyasta Geotama Indonesia
Key Responsibilities
  • Geostatistical Analysis
  • Geostatistical Modeling
  • Validation Model
  • Technical Reporting and Communication

Conducted spatial analysis of environmental pollution resulting from oil palm plantation activities using topography-controlled convergent interpolation. Analyzed waste concentration patterns from soil and water sample data, integrating terrain influence to better understand pollutant distribution. Applied geostatistical methods to enhance spatial accuracy and identify contamination trends shaped by landscape features. The results supported the creation of risk maps for environmental monitoring and sustainable land-use planning.
